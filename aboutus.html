About Us

Bacoor Branch

Address: FYNN Commercial Bldg., Gen. E. Aguinaldo Hwy., Bacoor City, Cavite.

Telephone: (046) 8970-7898

Email: aics_bacoor@aics.edu.ph

Located along one of the major hi-ways of Cavite, our Bacoor branch serves senior high school and college students offering ICT, Entrepreneurship, Computer Science, and other undergraduate programs.

Our Mission

To provide quality-oriented and globally-relevant IT and business education that empowers students to become lifelong learners and productive members of society.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution in IT and business education, recognized for innovative teaching, student success, and community impact.

Our History

The Asian Institute of Computer Studies (AICS) was founded in 1996, and expanded with the Bacoor branch among its southern-area branches.

As of today, AICS has multiple branches across Luzon and the Visayas, serving thousands of students and offering senior high school, undergraduate, and short IT certification programs.
